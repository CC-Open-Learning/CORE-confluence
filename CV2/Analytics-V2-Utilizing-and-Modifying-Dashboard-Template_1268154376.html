CORE Framework : Analytics V2 Utilizing and Modifying Dashboard Template

Created by Richard Colbourne on Aug 12, 2024

Currently, co-op students are unable to publish dashboards. Your full-time team leader for your project can publish the dashboard to the VARLab DLX Analytics Power BI workspace. Power BI Desktop is also required in order to modify the dashboard and can be downloaded here: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/power-platform/products/power-bi/downloads

  1. Download and open the DLX Summary Template here: DLXSummaryTemplate.pbit

  2. Once opened in Power BI, click on the Home tab in the top menu bar. Then click Transform Data.

  1. The Transform Data window will appear. Click on Source in the right-side query settings tab.

  1. Ensure you can see the name of your project table in the table that appears. You may need to click the Refresh Preview button if you cannot see the table name.

  1. Once you are sure your table is available, click Navigation from the right-side query settings tab.

  1. In the Formula bar, change the Source name (in quotes) to match the name of your table. For example, if the current formula is = SOURCE{[Name=”sample-table”]}[Data] and your table was named “myTable” you would change sample-table to myTable ( = SOURCE{[Name=”myTable”]}[Data] ). Once you’ve updated the formula, click the checkmark to the left of the formula bar.

  1. Click the Refresh Preview button and the table data should update to the data from your table.

  1. Once the table data has updated and everything looks correct, click the Close & Apply button to exit the Transform Data window.

  1. You may get a yellow pop-up window at the top of the screen informing you of unapplied changes. Click apply changes and your dashboard will update. Save the dashboard to finish the process.

Congrats, the template is now set up and you can begin creating your own custom metrics!
