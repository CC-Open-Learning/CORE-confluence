Breakfast & Learn

This modal example was part of a breakfast and learn session to introduce new team members to UI Toolkit, and VELCRO. The video has been split into an introduction, UI, and scripting sections and uploaded to SharePoint as Confluence has a 100MB video limit. The goal was to create the modal below with a basic script. Not everyone will be familiar with the project, or specific piece of UI, but the learning session is still very useful to showcase the expected workflow when working with UI Toolkit/VELCRO in DLX.
