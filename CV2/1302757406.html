1.0.0

W25-5

Launch of VELCRO 1.0.0. VELCRO will be more stable from here on out. This contains all UI designed up until December 2024. All previously implemented scripts/templates have been reviewed, and updated with the exception of the progress indicators/tables. This is due to them being in-use on the RT/TPI projects, and not wanting to introduce breaking changes to an in-flight project that still requires VELCRO updates. They will be updated when the project ships. They have been marked as [OUTDATED] in both confluence and engine.

Scripts:

  • Most methods in UIHelper.cs, and StyleHelper.cs were converted to TextElement and VisualElement extension methods. Functionality was maintained, but definitions changed. Projects will need to change syntax when calling methods BREAKING CHANGES 

//BEFORE
UIHelper.Show(Root);

//AFTER
Root.Show();
