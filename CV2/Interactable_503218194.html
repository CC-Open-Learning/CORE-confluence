CORE Framework : Interactable

Created by Geyang Liu, last modified on Aug 05, 2022

The IInteractable interface allows player to directly interact with objects in PC mode. It contains an Interact method which defines how the player can interact with the object, either with or without the involvement of a hand.

In PC mode, it is difficult for players to let the hand perform complex movements such as reaching out to the position of an object. In turn, the player can just click on the object with the mouse and trigger the Interact method. Most objectâ€™s Interact method will just let the hand grab the object. Some objects might perform unique actions such as changing states.
