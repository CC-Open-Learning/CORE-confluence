CORE Framework : Examples

Created by Geyang Liu, last modified on Aug 18, 2022

There are two example implementations of VR vehicle.

Jetbike

In VR mode, Grab on to the left handle to board the vehicle and press the secondary button on the left controller to unboard.

Use the joystick on the left controller for thrust and roll. Use the joystick on the right controller for turn and yaw. You need to grab on to the handles to register input.

In PC mode, left click on the vehicle to board and right click to unboard. Use the WASD keys for thrust and roll and use the mouse for turn and yaw.

Kart

The kart uses an alternative approach for VR vehicles. Instead of taking input from the controllers, it uses a physical wheel and lever to control.

In VR mode, grab the lever to board and press the secondary button on the left controller to unboard. Turn the steering wheel and lever to control the kart.

This implementation does not support PC mode.
