CORE Framework : DLX Analytics Metrics

Created by Jacky Lam on Jun 15, 2023

Overview

This documentation outlines the metrics we track from our DLX simulation. This also gives a direction of what events and data should be capture from the game to derive the metrics described.

Metrics

Below are the metrics we are currently tracking from our DLX simulation.

Unique Users

The Unique Users metrics tracks number of unique users (learner) who have utilized the DLX simulation since release.

Total Instructional Hours

The Total Instructional Hours metrics is tracking the collective hours that users spent in an DLX.

Average Session Time

The Average Session Time metrics is tracking how much time does an user spend on average in a DLX session.

Average Completion Time

The Average Completion Time metrics is tracking how much time does an user spend to complete the entire DLX simulation.

Completion Rate

The Completion Rate metrics tracks the percentage of completion of the DLX simulation.
